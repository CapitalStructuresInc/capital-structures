### Appointment

The Board of Directors of Capital Structures Inc. (the "Company"), acting by unanimous written consent of its sole director, hereby appoints:

**Audrey Chen**
**Title:** Head of Operations

Effective as of the date of this memo.

### Responsibilities

The Head of Operations shall be responsible for:

1. Coordination of structure documentation workflows between agents and counsel
2. Review of agent-generated documents prior to CEO approval
3. Management of filing deadlines and compliance calendar
4. Liaison with external service providers (registered agent, accounting, legal)
5. Coordination with verse.works on partnership deliverables
6. Maintenance of physical and digital archives
7. Operational support for exhibitions and public presentations

### Reporting

The Head of Operations reports directly to the Chief Executive Officer.

### Compensation

Compensation terms are documented separately under the Company's standard employment arrangements.

Ms. Chen serves concurrently as an employee of andrepeat.com (Martin Grasser, principal). This arrangement is disclosed. The Company acknowledges the affiliated-party nature of this appointment and notes it for the corporate record.
