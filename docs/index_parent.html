The company is the work.

The balance sheet is the artwork.

Capital Structures, Inc. turns participation into treasury, treasury into deployments, deployments into structures, and structures into public record.

Risk is not erased. It is transferred, isolated, repriced, deferred, ring-fenced, guaranteed, made non-recourse, or disclosed. Every structure here is a record of where risk was sent and under what terms it was permitted to arrive.

Participants capitalize the parent company. The board uses that capital to operate the institution, build its archive, stage its public life, and form internal structures only when they change something real about ownership, liability, cash flow, servicing, or reporting.

One parent issuer. One participant base. One treasury. One archive. One public institution. Purpose structures formed only when they perform a real operating function.

Archive

The archive is the institutional memory of the work and the place where structures are tested.

Each archived structure may be read in four views: unconsolidated — what the structure is permitted to say about itself. Consolidated — what the structure looks like when consequence paths are allowed to speak. Base case — authorized, funded, disclosed. Trigger state — contingent conditions active, the diagram is in motion.

Operations

Method

Capital Structures, Inc. is a single public institution, but its structures are still read in multiple conditions.

On an unconsolidated basis, a structure appears according to its formal legal boundaries, stated obligations, and authorized separations.

On a consolidated basis, the same arrangement is read across guarantees, dependencies, contingent support, servicing needs, and consequence paths that persist beyond those boundaries.

Where available, each structure is also shown in two states.

Base Case records the arrangement as authorized, funded, and disclosed. Triggers are dormant. Trigger State records what activates when a shortfall, guarantee event, liquidity event, maturity event, dissolution event, counterparty failure, governance action, or other contractual condition causes the diagram to move.

These are not alternate interpretations. They are alternate tests of the same structure.

A published structure should look cleaner on an unconsolidated basis than on a consolidated basis, and calmer in base case than in trigger state. If neither picture changes, it is not yet a structure. It is only a document.

Standards

A structure does not abolish exposure. It determines where exposure appears, under what name, at what cost, with what recourse, and at what distance from the originating entity.

Exposure is conserved. Risk displaced from one layer of a structure reappears elsewhere.

Disclosure is not transparency. A structure may be fully documented and still remain difficult to see.

The diagram is an argument. Every arrow, guarantee, exclusion, reserve, and trigger path is part of the disclosed arrangement.

Failure

The company may allow individual structures to fail economically, but not invisibly. Failure is not the opposite of the work. Hidden failure is.

A structure is allowed to fail. The failure must become document, ledger, and diagram.

Level 1
Operating Failure
A contract underperforms. A release does not monetize. An exhibition loses money. A reserve proves insufficient. Ordinary and disclosable without entity collapse.
Level 2
Structure Failure
A purpose structure becomes impaired, cannot support its obligations, triggers a contingency, or must be wound down. Acceptable if the failure path was documented, the residual exposure was legible, and the consequences are recorded in the archive.
Level 3
Institutional Failure
The parent cannot maintain reporting, servicing, archive continuity, or governance integrity. A higher threshold. The failure is no longer project-level; it threatens the continuity of the institution itself.
Level 4
Intentional Dissolution
The board decides the company is structurally complete and adopts dissolution as an artistic and legal end state. Not failure in the ordinary sense. A terminal governance action.

Formation Queue

Ideas are marked and tracked before they become structures. A marked idea is an administrative fact in waiting. Not every idea should become a company or series. The board uses the least elaborate tool that still changes something real.

Idea 001 — Participation Series Proposed

Admit participants into a specific work-structure or release-specific capital formation. A separate participation field with its own rights package, waterfall, trigger set, or registry.

Why it could be a structure

New ownership boundary, new reporting unit, new cash flow logic, new transfer regime.

Why it might only be a schedule first

If the participation class is still entirely parent-level and no new asset pool exists.

Idea 002 — Exhibition Structure Proposed

Isolate a major exhibition’s production budget, servicing obligations, sponsorship flows, and downside.

Could begin as

Operating spend + exhibition reserve + contract.

Matures into a structure when

Third-party capital, liabilities, or dedicated revenues become material.

Idea 003 — Archive Custody / Preservation Vehicle Proposed

Hold or administer core archival assets, documents, originals, or institutional records under a preservation mandate. Use when archive preservation becomes a discrete obligation with a different duration than ordinary operations.

Could begin as

Custody agreement + archive policy + reserve.

Idea 004 — Trigger Support Structure Proposed

Isolate a support pool intended to absorb defined trigger events across selected structures. Use when parent-level support needs explicit boundaries and the company wants to disclose support without turning every obligation into a general parent promise.

Note

Strong conceptually, but only justified if the support really exists.

Idea 005 — Registry / Transfer Administration Proposed

Formalize how participant records, transfer restrictions, secondary approvals, and unit movement are maintained. Use when participation transfer becomes real rather than speculative.

Probably begins as

Policy + schedule + service contract. Not an entity unless the servicing burden becomes real enough.

Idea 006 — Research Acquisition Structure Proposed

Acquire, license, or hold source materials, research archives, diagrams, books, or external document sets that become inputs to the work. Use when the company is buying a coherent body of materials where provenance and rights matter independently.

Idea 007 — Warehouse / Origination Series Proposed

Temporarily hold in-process ideas, acquired rights, draft contracts, or pre-issuance material before final deployment. Lets the company form, mark, and abandon ideas publicly.

Why this is interesting

It makes the company’s creative process — formation, evaluation, abandonment — part of the disclosed record.

Idea 008 — Dissolution Reserve / End-State Structure Proposed

Explicitly prepare for the possibility that the company ends while documents, rights, and archival obligations continue. Use when the project wants its ending to be as structured as its growth.

Note

This may be one of the most important later structures.

Glossary

Operating Company Definition

An operating business whose core activity is originating, administering, documenting, publishing, exhibiting, contracting, governing, and reporting structures through documents, agreements, policies, and controlled vehicle formation.

Purpose Structure Definition

A backstage operating instrument formed only when the company needs a new boundary around ownership, liability, servicing, cash flow, reserve support, or reporting. If deleting the structure changes only the poetry, it should not be a legal entity.

Participant Definition

A holder of parent-level securities. Participants capitalize the parent company. They are not buying a separate art project. They are participating in one company whose public life is the work.

Treasury / Formation Reserve Definition

The central pool of participant capital at the parent. Used to fund operations, build the archive, produce documents and interfaces, support exhibitions, create reserves, and capitalize purpose structures when justified. Participants are not merely listed. They are booked.

Board Deployment Definition

Any authorized use of treasury or policy authority by the board. Most deployments are not entity formation. They are operating spend, contracts, reserves, schedules, and policies. Vehicle formation is the last tool, not the first.

Structural Fiduciary Definition

The Company’s modified fiduciary standard comprising Truthful Disclosure, Procedural Integrity, and Structural Good Faith. Management decisions may include forming entities with no revenue-generating purpose, entering economically neutral transactions, maintaining excess complexity, and dissolving the Company at a moment determined to be structurally complete.

Unconsolidated Definition

What the structure is permitted to say about itself. The view bounded by formal legal separations, stated recourse, and authorized entity lines.

Consolidated Definition

What the structure looks like when guarantees, dependencies, support expectations, and consequence paths are allowed to speak across those boundaries.

Base Case Definition

Authorized, funded, disclosed. Triggers dormant.

Trigger State Definition

Contingent conditions active. The diagram is in motion.

Residual Exposure Definition

Risk that remains after transfer, isolation, or ring-fencing. What the structure cannot fully exile.

Exposure Conservation Definition

The proposition that risk displaced from one layer of a structure must reappear elsewhere rather than disappear. A doctrine of structural thermodynamics.

Failure Doctrine Definition

The company may allow individual structures to fail economically, but not invisibly. A structure is allowed to fail. The failure must become document, ledger, and diagram.

Formation Queue Definition

The disclosed pipeline of ideas under consideration. Ideas are marked (proposed, reserved, authorized, active, dormant, triggered, impaired, dissolved, archived) before they become structures. A marked idea is an administrative fact in waiting.

Purpose Structure Law Definition

A purpose structure may be formed only when it performs a real function that cannot be adequately performed by operating spend, contract, reserve, schedule, or policy. If deleting the structure changes only the poetry, it should not be a legal entity.
