Authorization of Structure 003: Typeface License Agreement

Capital Structures, Inc. (the “Company”) deploys the typeface family known as “188 Sans” across all public interfaces, governance instruments, diagrams, and published structures as a core element of the Company’s institutional identity; and

the Typeface was designed by Martin Grasser at And Repeat, Inc. (“And Repeat”), a California corporation of which Martin Grasser is the principal. And Repeat is the sole owner of all intellectual property rights in the Typeface; and

Martin Grasser serves simultaneously as Chief Executive Officer and sole Director of the Company and as the principal of And Repeat. This dual role constitutes a conflict of interest that is intentional, disclosed, and documented on the face of this Resolution; and

the Board has determined that formalizing the typeface license as a disclosed corporate structure serves the Company’s institutional and structural purposes. The license creates a documented cash flow between the Company and an external counterparty, establishes a recurring operating expense from treasury, and produces a diagrammable relationship that becomes part of the Company’s published architecture; and

the Board has reviewed the proposed terms — a non-exclusive, worldwide, perpetual license at an annual fee of $5,000 — and has determined that such terms are consistent with standard commercial typeface licensing for institutional use; and

the Board notes that the Typeface renders the documents that describe the structure that governs its use. This recursion is intentional and constitutes part of the disclosed work;

that the Board hereby authorizes the Company to enter into a Typeface License Agreement (TLA-001-2026) with And Repeat, Inc. for the license of the 188 Sans typeface family, on the terms set forth in the draft Agreement; and

that the annual License Fee of Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000) shall be classified as an operating expense and deployed from the Company’s treasury. Each payment shall be documented in the treasury deployment records and disclosed in periodic reporting; and

that this transaction shall be designated as Structure 003 in the Company’s archive and diagrammed in accordance with the Company’s documentation standards; and

that the Board has reviewed the conflict of interest arising from Martin Grasser’s dual role pursuant to Delaware General Corporation Law §144 and has determined the transaction to be in the best interests of the Company as defined under the Structural Fiduciary; and

that this resolution shall be published in full on the Company’s website and archived in the Company’s governance records.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned has executed this Resolution as of the date set forth below.

Martin Grasser
Sole Director and Chief Executive Officer
