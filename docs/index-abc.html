About

Capital Structures is a logistics company, moving standard financing vehicles and terms through existing market and legal infrastructure to clients at the lowest possible cost.

Every structure the Company builds — every series, every agreement, every instrument — is documented and published in full. The back office is open. The governance is public. The diagrams are the product.

The Company is a Delaware C-Corporation, formed in 2025. It may never generate profit and may intentionally dissolve at any time.

News

Capital Structures Inc. Announces Intent to Form

Summary

Capital Structures Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation, announces its formation as an intellectual property company whose sole product is the creation, execution, and disclosure of corporate and financial structures.

The Company intends to conduct a Regulation Crowdfunding (Reg CF) offering through a registered online portal. Through the offering, the Company expects to make up to 20 Class B Units available at an anticipated price of $500–$1,000 per unit, for a target raise of $100,000.

What Participants Receive

Each contribution creates a new Protected Series under Delaware law. Participants receive Class B Units — real securities, documented in the Company’s public archive. Class B Units carry:

  • No voting rights
  • No dividends
  • No liquidation preference
  • Position inside a fully disclosed corporate system

How to Participate

The offering will be conducted through a Reg CF portal (expected: Wefunder or Republic). The Company will announce the portal listing and open date when Form C has been filed with the SEC.

Interested parties may follow the Company’s progress at github.com/CapitalStructuresInc/capital-structures, where all operational documents, governance records, and structure diagrams are published.

Risk Factors

The Company may never generate revenue. The Company may intentionally dissolve at any time. Management is not incentivized to increase the value of any securities. Class B Units may have no resale market. Participants should expect total loss of their contribution.

Important Notice

No securities are currently being offered. This announcement does not constitute an offer to sell or a solicitation of an offer to buy any security. Any offering will be made only through a Reg CF portal in compliance with applicable securities laws, and only after a Form C has been filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

Structure 001 — Self-Dealing Architecture

The Company’s first structure. CEO Martin Grasser authorizes the creation of Protected Series 001 and personally acquires Class B Units, existing on both sides of the transaction. The parent company guarantees Series 001 performance. Self-dealing is disclosed on the face of all documentation.

Components

  • Delaware Protected Series 001 (under Capital Structures Inc. Series LLC)
  • Class B Units issued to Martin Grasser (personally)
  • Parent guarantee of Series performance
  • Full consolidation

Counterparties

Capital Structures Inc. — Parent / Guarantor
Martin Grasser (personally) — Unit Holder (affiliated: CEO of parent)

Self-Dealing Disclosure

Martin Grasser serves simultaneously as Chief Executive Officer authorizing the creation of the Series, sole director approving the transaction, and personal acquirer of Class B Units. This arrangement is intentional, disclosed, and documented.
