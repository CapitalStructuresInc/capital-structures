Historical Precedent. The self-dealing structure mirrors arrangements common in Enron-era SPE formations, particularly the LJM partnerships where CFO Andrew Fastow served as both Enron officer and fund manager. The critical difference: Fastow’s dual role was obscured through board waiver of conflict-of-interest policies. Here, the dual role is the stated purpose of the structure.

Press Release — Formation

Capital Structures Inc. Announces Intent to Form

Capital Structures Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation, announces its formation as an intellectual property company whose sole product is the creation, execution, and disclosure of corporate and financial structures.

The Company intends to conduct a Regulation Crowdfunding (Reg CF) offering through a registered online portal. Through the offering, the Company expects to make up to 20 Class B Units available at an anticipated price of $500–$1,000 per unit, for a target raise of $100,000.

What Participants Receive. Each contribution creates a new Protected Series under Delaware law. Participants receive Class B Units — real securities, documented in the Company’s public archive. Class B Units carry:

  • No voting rights
  • No dividends
  • No liquidation preference
  • Position inside a fully disclosed corporate system

How to Participate. The offering will be conducted through a Reg CF portal (expected: Wefunder or Republic). The Company will announce the portal listing and open date when Form C has been filed with the SEC.

Interested parties may follow the Company’s progress at github.com/CapitalStructuresInc/capital-structures, where all operational documents, governance records, and structure diagrams are published.

Risk Factors. The Company may never generate revenue. The Company may intentionally dissolve at any time. Management is not incentivized to increase the value of any securities. Class B Units may have no resale market. Participants should expect total loss of their contribution.

Important Notice. No securities are currently being offered. This announcement does not constitute an offer to sell or a solicitation of an offer to buy any security. Any offering will be made only through a Reg CF portal in compliance with applicable securities laws, and only after a Form C has been filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

Prepared by the Global Finance Department of Capital Structures Inc.
