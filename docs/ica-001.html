Article 3
Ongoing Development Obligation

3.1 Obligation. The Promissory Note constitutes advance consideration for the Contributor’s commitment to design and document all future Company structures, prepare all governance instruments, manage counterparty relationships, and perform all creative and operational activities necessary to realize the Company’s purpose (the “Ongoing Development Obligation”).

3.2 Scope. The Ongoing Development Obligation includes, without limitation:

(a) Design and documentation of all corporate and financial structures;

(b) Preparation and maintenance of governance documentation;

(c) Management of counterparty and distribution relationships;

(d) Supervision of AI agent infrastructure;

(e) All creative, operational, and administrative activities required to produce, execute, and disclose the Company’s structures.

3.3 Duration. The Ongoing Development Obligation continues for the life of the Company or until the Promissory Note is discharged, whichever occurs first.

(a) A liquidity event generating proceeds of $10,000,000 or more;

(e) The ten-year backstop date (the tenth anniversary of the Effective Date).

4.5 Non-Recourse. The Note is an obligation of Protected Series 001 only. The Contributor shall have no recourse to the parent company, to any other Protected Series, or to any other assets of the Company beyond the assets of Series 001.
